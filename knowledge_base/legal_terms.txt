Q: What is earnest money?
A: Earnest money is a deposit (typically 1-3% of purchase price) showing the buyer's serious intent.

Q: What contingencies are normal?
A: Common contingencies include: 1) Financing, 2) Inspection, 3) Appraisal, and 4) Home sale contingencies.

Q: What is a disclosure statement?
A: A legal document where sellers reveal known property defects or issues to potential buyers.

Q: What's the difference between escrow and closing?
A: Escrow is the holding period (30-45 days) where terms are met, while closing is the final signing and fund transfer.
