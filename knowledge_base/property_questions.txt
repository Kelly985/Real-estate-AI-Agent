Q: What documents do I need to sell my house?
A: You'll need: 1) Property deed, 2) Mortgage information, 3) Property tax records, 4) Home inspection reports, and 5) Any warranties.

Q: How do you determine listing price?
A: We perform a comparative market analysis considering: 1) Recent sales, 2) Current listings, 3) Property condition, and 4) Local market trends.

Q: What's the average time to sell a home?
A: In our current market, properties typically sell within 30-45 days when priced correctly.

Q: Do you handle rental properties?
A: Yes, we manage both long-term rentals and vacation properties with different service packages available.
